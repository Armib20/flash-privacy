Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Flash ("we", "our", "the app") is a climbing project tracking app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account information:

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Password (stored securely as a bcrypt hash — we never store or see your plain-text password)

Climbing data you create:

  • Projects (name, grade, color, deadline, cover image)
  • Sessions (start/end time, location, participants)
  • Attempts (send and flash records, notes)
  • Groups (name, description, member roles)

Media:

  • Photos and videos you upload (avatars, cover images, group posts)

Device information:

  • Push notification token (if you enable notifications)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the app (tracking your climbing projects, sessions, and progress)
  • To send you notifications (session invites, deadline reminders, activity nudges) if you opt in
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
  • To enable group features (sharing progress with your climbing partners)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS S3 & CloudFront): Stores and delivers media files you upload (photos, videos). Data is stored in the US.
  • Expo Push Notifications: Delivers push notifications to your device. We share your device push token with Expo's notification service.
  • Resend: Sends transactional emails (verification, password reset). We share your email address with Resend for this purpose.

We do not sell your data to any third party. We do not use any analytics or advertising services.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Account data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway.
  • Media files are stored in AWS S3 and served via CloudFront CDN.
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text.
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens with a 30-day expiry.
  • All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS.

5. Data Sharing

Your climbing data (projects, sessions, attempts) is visible to members of groups you join. Your name and avatar are visible to other group members. We do not share your data with any other parties beyond the third-party services listed above.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data — all your climbing data is visible in the app.
  • Update your data — edit your profile, projects, and sessions at any time.
  • Delete your account — you can permanently delete your account from within the app. This removes all your personal data, climbing records, and uploaded media. Groups you own will be transferred to the next admin or member, or deleted if you are the only member.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data (profile, climbing records, media files) is permanently deleted from our database and storage services.

8. Children's Privacy

Flash is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
