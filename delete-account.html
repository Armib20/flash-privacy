Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Flash account and all associated data directly from the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Flash app and log in to your account.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Edit Profile.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, avatar)
  • All climbing projects you created
  • All session records and attempt history
  • All photos and videos you uploaded
  • Your group memberships and posts
  • All notification history

What happens to groups you own

  • If your group has other admins, ownership is transferred to the longest-standing admin.
  • If there are no admins but there are members, ownership is transferred to the longest-standing member.
  • If you are the only member, the group and all its data are deleted.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Data retention

Account deletion is immediate. All personal data, climbing records, and media files are permanently removed from our database and storage services. No data is retained after deletion.

Need help?

If you are unable to access your account and need assistance with deletion, contact us at flashclimbingapp@gmail.com.
